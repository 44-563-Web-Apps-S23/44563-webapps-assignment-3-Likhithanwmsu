LAVENDER CHEETAH

My superhero is LAVENDER CHEETAH. The Lavender Cheetah is the fastest terrestrial mammal on the planet and the most vulnerable big cat in Africa. The Cheetah is specially adapted for speed and can accelerate from 0 to 110 km/h in less than three seconds. Their stride is seven meters long when moving at peak speed.The following are the super powers of the Cheetas:

  1. The planet's fastest land animal is built for super speed, rather than stamina.
  2. When they do stir, cheetahs can accelerate faster than most sports cars.
  3. Cheetahs could be sprinting towards extinction.
